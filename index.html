Everything is Easy When You Have OK Wallet




Add Money


  Log In to OK Wallet App.

  Choose Add Money menu.

  Select your preferable option to add money like Debit or Credit card/Other bank account/Internet banking.

  Enter required information and your desired amount.

  Enter your 4-digit PIN to confirm the transaction.

  And you will receive a confirmation SMS of successful transaction.



Cash-In


  Visit your nearest OK Wallet Agent point or OBL Branch.

  Communicate your desire cash in amount to Agent/ OK service manager.

  Write down OK Wallet Number and the Cash-In amount in the transaction book or OK Deposit Slip.

  Pay the cash amount of money you want to Cash-In.

  You will be notified by confirmation SMS from ONE Bank Ltd (16269) of successful Cash-In.



Money Transfer


  Log In to OK Wallet App.

  Choose Money transfer.

  Enter recipient’s OK A/C, OBL A/C or Other A/C number.

  Enter the amount you wish to transfer.

  Enter your 4-digit PIN to confirm the transaction.

  And you will receive a confirmation SMS of successful transfer.



Utility Bill Payment


  Dial *269# from USSD menu or go to OK Wallet App.

  Choose payment option and select your bill payment provider.

  Choose the Bill/Fee details and payable amount will appear.

  Enter your 4-digit PIN to confirm the transaction.

  And you will get the confirmation SMS of successful bill payment.



Cash-Out


  Choose your preferable OK Wallet Agent/ OBL Branch/ATM.

  Follow Cash-Out process for Agent/ OBL Branch/ATM.

  And you will get the confirmation SMS of successful transfer.


Mobile Recharge


  Select Mobile Recharge option,

  Select your mobile operator.

  Select Prepaid or Postpaid.

  Enter the phone number for recharge.

  Enter the amount you want to recharge.

  Enter your 4-digit PIN.

  Press confirm and your mobile recharge will be successful.



Merchant Payment


  Dial *269# from USSD menu or go to OK Wallet App,

  Select Merchant Payment option,

  Scan the QR Code or Enter Merchant A/C number,

  Enter the payment amount,

  Enter your 4-digit PIN to confirm the transaction,

  And you will get a confirmation SMS of successful payment.
